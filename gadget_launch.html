DRONE:A "drone" refers to an unmanned aircraft (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle or UAV) that is controlled remotely or flies autonomously using a software-controlled flight plan and onboard sensors. Drones are used in a wide range of military and civilian applications, such as surveillance, photography, delivery,and agriculture, and they are also an aircraft without a pilot,a continuous low noise, and a male bee in a social bee colony

# Need of our project

Agriculture: Drones assist in precision farming by mapping fields, monitoring crop health, and applying fertilizers or pesticides more efficiently.
Aerial Photography & Videography: Drones have revolutionized film, real estate, and travel by making it easier and more affordable to capture stunning aerial views and cinematic shots.
Infrastructure Inspection: They are used to inspect large and hazardous structures like bridges, wind turbines, power lines, and oil pipelines, providing detailed images from unique vantage points.
Public Safety & Search and Rescue: Drones help law enforcement, firefighters, and search and rescue teams by monitoring large areas, locating missing persons, and providing real-time situational awareness during emergencies.
Delivery & Logistics:Companies are increasingly using drones for cargo and medical deliveries, offering efficient and cost-effective solutions for transporting goods to hard-to-reach or remote areas.
